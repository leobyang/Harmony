ACUPUNCTURE

Acupuncture is a therapeutic metho which inserts the fine needles into a patient's body at various specific points along the energy pathways called meridians to prevent and treat a wide range of health problems. Acupuncture is proved to be an effective, safe and complementary to medications or even surgery for a variety of conditions and illnesses. Our clinic uses only high quality sterile single use needles.

TRADITIONAL CHINESE HERBAL MEDICINE (TCM)

Traditional Chinese Herbal Medicine, one of the oldest continually practiced medicines, uses the natural properties (cold, cool, hot, warm, neutral) and flavours (pungent, sour, sweet, salty, bitter and bland) of natural medicinal substances to restore and balance the entire body. It is prescribed as a standard remedy or modified remedy to suit the individual patient's condition.
